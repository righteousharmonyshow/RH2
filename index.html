Righteous Harmony

Righteous Harmony is a heartfelt, high‑energy tribute to the legendary Righteous Brothers. This concert celebrates their soaring harmonies and iconic chart‑toppers, plus the artists who inspired them — and the modern voices they inspired in turn.

From soul and rock to contemporary pop, we bridge eras with powerful vocals, tight harmony, and a whole lot of heart.
